# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment‑free experience.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include respect, empathy, and constructive feedback.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and public spaces.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the maintainers.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Consequences may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal.

## Attribution
This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant.
